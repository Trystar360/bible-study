ℹ️ About Berean

Berean helps small-group leaders, teachers, and anyone curious prepare a Bible study fast. Describe a topic in your own words and Berean matches it against a hand-built topical library, then assembles verses, discussion questions, a timed outline, leader tips, and trusted external resources.

Named for the Bereans of Acts 17:11, who “received the word with all readiness of mind, and searched the scriptures daily, whether those things were so.”

Features

  • 🔍 Natural-language topic search with synonym matching and verse-reference lookup
  • 📖 Live verse text (public-domain translations via bible-api.com), plus one-click links to your preferred translation on BibleGateway and Blue Letter Bible
  • 🧭 Study outline generator with a timed agenda tuned to your group length, size, audience, and format
  • 💬 Discussion questions structured as Opening → Observation → Interpretation → Application
  • 🎯 Icebreakers, activities, leader tips, and prayer prompts per topic
  • ⭐ Save studies, bookmark verses, and keep private notes (stored locally in your browser)
  • 🖨️ Print-ready study guides, Markdown export, copy-to-clipboard, and shareable links
  • 🌙 Dark mode, verse of the day, related-topic exploration, and a full topic browser

A note on sources

In-page verse text uses public-domain translations (World English Bible, KJV, ASV, BBE). Links to NIV, ESV, NLT, and other modern translations open on BibleGateway. External resource links (GotQuestions, BibleProject, Blue Letter Bible, OpenBible, Desiring God) are provided for further research; Berean isn't affiliated with them.

Open source, static, and free. No tracking, no account, no server — your notes never leave your device.
